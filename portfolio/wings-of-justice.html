Salvation awaits.

Kayle and Morgana are demi-goddesses that represent the divine power of justice. More importantly, they’re playable in League of Legends. This cinematic trailer was created for their release in Wild Rift.

It’s also one of the first things I wrote at Riot, plus my first time ever writing any voice-over. I still can’t believe they let me do that—thanks Tori and Bill!

Role: I wrote the script, with a lot of help from creative director Bill Barteldes and a few other writers.

Release date: December 2, 2021
