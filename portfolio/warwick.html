Run... while you can.

Players have known Warwick since 2009, when League of Legends was released. To introduce him to Wild Rift, we wanted to explore a new side of his character.

Role: This script was a team effort between me, Dylan Bates, Bethany Rock, and Tori Fica. I wrote our mysterious narrator’s voiceover, and contributed eye-related thoughts.

Release date: November 22, 2022
