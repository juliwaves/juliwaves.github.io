New adventures!

In 2019, Riot announced Riot Forge—a publishing label that would partner with other studios to develop more games in the League of Legends universe.

Two years later, we gave players a huge update on our progress. I helped with surprise-dropping two new titles (Hextech Mayhem and Ruined King) and announcing another (Song of Nunu).

Role: With Bethany Higa, I wrote:

  • The creative platform for the Riot Forge Showcase video. (Video script by Erika Haas and Nikki Brown!)
  • Website copy for Song of Nunu.
  • Trailer copy for Hextech Mayhem.
  • Trailer copy for Ruined King.

Release date: November 16, 2021
