  • creating space for marginalized or underrepresented people in my hobbies.
  • connecting the amazing people i know.
  • making shit happen.

in that vein, i currently run:

  • MAGICAL in Gaming, a group for alumni of marginalized-gender collegiate gaming clubs
  • 2XDG, a marginalized gender 2XKO discord server
  • a wide variety of in-person and online activities for the people in my life. i.e. "i make my friends hang out with me a lot"

previously, i ran:

  • cal marginalized genders in gaming, a mar-gen gaming club at uc berkeley
  • the college game jam, a game jam for college students across the globe
